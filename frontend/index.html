What it does

One coach, every layer of training — live in your browser.

👻

Movement Twin

Your best rep becomes your baseline: a ghost skeleton to race, and measured deviations — “torso leaned 13° more than your best” — whenever you fall short of yourself.

🗣

Voice in, voice out

Spoken coaching cues, hands-free commands, and real conversation: ask “what should I fix first?” mid-set and hear the answer. Designed for eyes-free training.

📉

Fatigue detection

When form degrades rep after rep, the coach names the exact rep the decline began — and tells you to rest before bad reps pile up.

🏅

5 drills, every rep measured

Strength (squat, push-up, curl, shoulder press with lockout & back-arch safety check) and conditioning (jumping jacks with a full-extension check) — each drill has its own joint-angle state machine.

🖥

Coach any footage

Upload a video, or screen-capture a playing YouTube clip — the same engine overlays the skeleton and coaches the athlete live.

🤖

4 AI agents + local LLM

Biomechanics, injury-risk, programming and progress agents write an explainable readiness report — generated by Claude or a fully local LLM via Ollama.

👤

Zero-knowledge accounts

Email + password sign-in where the password never leaves the device and your history is AES-256 encrypted. No server, no database, nothing to breach.

🧑‍🏫

Team dashboard

Athletes train privately in their own browsers; the coach sees sessions, trends and risk flags for the whole squad behind an access key.

How it works

1

Pose estimation

Google MediaPipe (full model) tracks 33 body landmarks at ~30fps, entirely on your device via WebAssembly + GPU.

2

Biomechanics engine

Joint angles run through per-exercise state machines with EMA smoothing, anti-blip guards and visibility gating.

3

Form scoring

Each rep starts at 100 and loses points for depth, alignment and stability faults — flagging common form-risk patterns. A coaching aid, not a medical diagnostic tool.

4

The Twin

Your best rep’s metrics become your private baseline; every rep is measured against it, and deviations are spoken in degrees.

🔒

Privacy by architecture

Your camera feed physically cannot leave the browser — a live counter proves it, and DevTools verifies it. Local-only mode sends nothing at all.
