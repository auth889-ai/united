How it works

1

Pose estimation

Google MediaPipe tracks 33 body landmarks at ~30fps, entirely on your device via WebAssembly + GPU.

2

Biomechanics engine

Joint angles (hip–knee–ankle, shoulder–elbow–wrist, body line) run through a per-exercise state machine that detects reps and phases.

3

Form scoring

Each rep starts at 100 and loses points for depth, alignment and stability faults — the same cues a physio checks.

4

Voice in, voice out

Faults are spoken aloud, and the app listens back — "start", "switch to squats", "how am I doing" — so the entire workout works eyes-free, including for blind and low-vision athletes.
