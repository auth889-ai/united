# Presentation script — WHAT → WHY → HOW — target 3:45 (limit 5:00)

**Before recording:** app :8000, backend :8001, Memobase :8019, Ollama running
(`curl localhost:8001/api/health`). 1–2 practice sessions under YOUR name +
wait a few minutes (coach needs memory). Test once: "Coach, what do you
remember about me?" OBS system audio ON; camera hip height, 2–3 m back, full
body in frame. Architecture diagram open in a tab.

---

## WHAT — 0:00–0:40 (open on you mid-squat, app visible)

DO: one shallow squat in the first 10 seconds so the coach says "go deeper".

> "This is FormCoach AI.
> An AI sports coach that lives in your browser.
> It watches me train. It counts my reps. It scores my form.
> And it just corrected me — out loud.
> No screen. No touching. Just a coach."

## WHY — 0:40–1:20 (face to camera)

> "Why did I build this?
> Because most gym injuries come from bad form.
> A real coach costs too much.
> And the apps? They want money every month. They need internet.
> And they send your video to their servers.
> I wanted a coach that is private, free, offline, hands-free —
> and one that remembers me.
> So I built it."

## HOW, Part A — 1:20–2:50 (see it work — squats, one take)

1. Say **"squats"** … **"start"**. Touch nothing.
   > "Everything by voice."
2. 1 perfect squat → "Personal best captured" → 1 bad squat → hear the degrees.
   > "It learned my best rep. Now it compares me — to me.
   > Not to some random standard."
3. Ask: **"Coach, what do you remember about me?"** Let it answer fully.
   > "It remembers. My mistakes. My records. My goal.
   > That is the Memobase memory — running on my own laptop."
4. Point at the privacy counter.
   > "And here is the proof of privacy:
   > thousands of frames analyzed. Zero video uploaded."
5. Say **"finish"** → open the flip-book, turn 2 pages.
   > "Every session becomes a book. My mistakes, photographed. The coach's verdict."
6. Fast cuts (5s): 2 push-ups → 2 jacks.
   > "And it's five drills — squats, push-ups, curls, shoulder press, jumping jacks."

## HOW, Part B — 2:50–3:30 (how it works inside — the diagram)

DO: point box by box, one line per box.

> "Inside, it is simple.
> The camera finds 33 points on my body, 30 times a second.
> A physics engine turns them into reps and scores.
> Only numbers leave the browser. Never video.
> Then four AI agents study my session — technique, injury, plan, progress —
> running on Llama, through Ollama. Free and local.
> And Memobase saves my long-term memory.
> All of it on one laptop. All of it tested — all green."

## CLOSE — 3:30–3:45 (face to camera, smile)

> "Private. Free. Offline. Hands-free. And it remembers you.
> FormCoach AI — every athlete deserves a coach. Now everyone has one."
