# Short presentation script — simple English — target 3:45 (limit 5:00)

**Before recording:** app :8000, backend :8001, Memobase Docker :8019, Ollama
all running (`curl localhost:8001/api/health`). Do 1–2 practice sessions under
YOUR name first, wait a few minutes, and test once: "Coach, what do you
remember about me?" — it must answer out loud. OBS: system audio ON, camera at
hip height, 2–3 m back, full body visible. Architecture diagram open in a tab.

---

## 0:00–0:30 — 1 · Problem (face to camera)

> "Most gym injuries come from bad form.
> A real coach costs too much.
> Fitness apps want money, accounts, and internet.
> And the AI apps send your video to their servers.
> That is not okay."

## 0:30–1:00 — 2 · Solution: five promises (count on fingers)

> "So I built FormCoach AI. It runs in your browser.
> It is **private** — your video never leaves your device.
> It is **free** — no API key, no payment.
> It works **offline**.
> It is **hands-free** — you talk, it talks back.
> And it **remembers you** — with a Memobase memory on my own laptop."

## 1:00–2:30 — 3 · Demo: squats (one continuous take)

1. Say **"squats"** … **"start"**. Touch nothing.
   > "I did not touch anything. Voice in, voice out."
2. 2 good squats, then 1 shallow → coach says "go deeper".
   > "It counts. It scores. And it just corrected me — out loud."
3. 1 perfect squat → "Personal best captured" → 1 bad squat → hear the degrees.
   > "It learned my best rep. Now it compares me… to me."
4. Ask: **"Coach, what do you remember about me?"** Let it answer fully.
   > "That is the memory. My mistakes. My records. My goal.
   > All saved on my laptop."
5. Point at the privacy counter.
   > "Thousands of frames analyzed. Zero video uploaded."
6. Say **"finish"** → open the flip-book → turn 2 pages.
   > "And every session becomes a book."

## 2:30–2:50 — 4 · Other drills (fast cuts: 2 push-ups → 1 press → 2 jacks)

> "Not only squats. Push-ups. Curls. Shoulder press. Jumping jacks.
> It can even coach a YouTube video."

## 2:50–3:30 — 5 · Architecture (screen: the diagram, point box by box)

> "Everything runs on my laptop. Nothing is in the cloud.
> The camera finds 33 points on my body, 30 times a second.
> The physics engine counts reps and scores my form.
> Only numbers go to the server. Never video.
> Four AI agents study my session — technique, injury, plan, progress.
> They run on Llama, through Ollama. Free and local.
> And Memobase builds my memory profile.
> All tested. All green."

## 3:30–3:45 — 6 · Close (face to camera, smile)

> "Private. Free. Offline. Hands-free. And it remembers you.
> FormCoach AI — every athlete deserves a coach. Now everyone has one."
